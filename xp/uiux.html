UI/UX Design — BeingHR

Redesigning HR platform experiences with a scalable design system.

Problem

BeingHR's HR management platform required improvements to user experience consistency, design scalability, and developer handoff efficiency. Existing flows contained friction points, and the product lacked a sufficiently standardised design system to support future growth across devices and use cases.

Actions Taken

As a UI/UX Design Intern, I conducted product analysis across existing workflows and redesigned key user journeys to improve usability and consistency. I redesigned seven platform pages and fourteen user flows while collaborating with internal stakeholders to align business objectives with user needs.

I created reusable design assets, standardised interface components, and developed responsive prototypes that could be referenced during implementation. Particular attention was given to reducing design inconsistencies across web and mobile breakpoints and improving the efficiency of the design-to-development process.

Results

  • Delivered redesigned experiences across multiple product areas
  • Produced a library of 25 reusable Figma components that established a scalable design foundation
  • Responsive prototypes validated with internal stakeholders
  • Improved communication between design and development teams
  • Standardised component approach reduced repetitive design work and accelerated handoff processes
