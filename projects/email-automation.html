Workflow Automation

Email Automation System (RPA)

A UiPath-based RPA solution that eliminates repetitive email workflows for university administrative staff managing 22 student clubs.

Problem

Manual weekly email workflow for 22 clubs: Saturday reminders, report follow-ups, signature correction requests, and account-switching between two email IDs. No centralised tracking across 65+ events per semester.

Solution

UiPath RPA bot connected to a central Google Sheets tracking spreadsheet. Status-based trigger logic: no event activity → send reminder; report awaited → send follow-up; signatures incomplete → send correction. Account-routing eliminates wrong-account sends.

Outcome

  • Stakeholder interview conducted and documented (Q&A Format, 14-Feb-2026)
  • 22 clubs, 65+ events per semester covered by the automation scope
  • 15+ automated email response templates for different status/trigger combinations
  • Solution validated by stakeholder as addressing the primary pain point
  • Weekly manual email drafting time reduced to near-zero
