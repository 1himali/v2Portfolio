SaaS / AI

Vibranium

AI-powered SaaS product intelligence workspace with market analysis, competitor discovery, and strategic insights.

Problem

Product teams lack a unified intelligence workspace to analyse market positioning, track competitor landscapes, and derive strategic insights for their SaaS products. Existing tools are fragmented and disconnected.

Solution

An authenticated multi-page SaaS intelligence dashboard with product profile setup, market intelligence analysis, product health KPIs, competitor discovery, strategic insights, and an AI assistant — all powered by Firebase auth/data layer and a FastAPI backend.

Outcome

  • Multi-page authenticated dashboard with 7-step product setup wizard
  • Real-time market analysis with sentiment scoring and feature comparison
  • Product health monitoring with DAU/MAU, revenue, and feature usage analytics
  • Competitor discovery with positioning scatter plots and market gap detection
  • AI assistant with chat interface for strategic recommendations

Technologies

React 18 Vite Tailwind CSS Framer Motion Recharts Firebase Auth Firestore FastAPI
